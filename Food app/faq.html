FAQs - Food Delivery

How do I place an order?

Browse the menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can track your order after confirmation.

Which areas do you deliver to?

We deliver in Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, and other major cities. Enter your address to check availability.

What are the delivery charges?

Charges vary by area and order size. You'll see the exact fee before checkout.

What payment methods are available?

We accept Cash on Delivery, Cards, Easypaisa, JazzCash, and Bank Transfers.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 5 minutes by contacting support.

What if my order is late or incorrect?

Contact support for a resolution or refund if your order is late or wrong.
